Campus Accessibility Navigator

A mobile navigation app to help students with visual impairments and disabilities navigate the CSULA campus via safe, accessible routes. The app provides turn-by-turn directions to building entrances with ramps, elevators, and accessible pathways while incorporating campus landmarks for easier wayfinding.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Valhalla routing engine, OpenStreetMap and Docker.
